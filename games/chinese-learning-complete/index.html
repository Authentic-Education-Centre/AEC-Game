🍎 Apple设备用户专用指南 / Apple Device User Guide

📱 如果听不到声音，请按以下步骤：

  • 🔇 检查静音开关：iPhone/iPad侧边的橙色开关要关闭
  • 🔊 调高音量：按音量+键，确保媒体音量开启
  • 👆 多次点击：朗读按钮可能需要点击2-3次才能激活
  • 🌐 换浏览器：强烈建议下载Chrome浏览器使用

🌐 If you can't hear audio, please follow these steps:

  • 🔇 Check silent switch: Turn off the orange switch on iPhone/iPad side
  • 🔊 Turn up volume: Press volume+ button, ensure media volume is on
  • 👆 Click multiple times: Audio buttons may need 2-3 clicks to activate
  • 🌐 Switch browser: Strongly recommend downloading Chrome browser
